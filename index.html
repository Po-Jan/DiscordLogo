SVG image

SVG images, or Scalable Vector Graphics, are ideal for web visuals due to their resolution independence. Unlike raster images, SVGs remain sharp at any size, making them perfect for logos, icons, and illustrations. They use XML to define shapes, allowing easy customization with CSS or JavaScript.

Bezier image

Bezier curves are mathematical curves widely used in graphics and design. Defined by control points, they create smooth, scalable paths ideal for vector illustrations. These curves enable precise control over shapes and transitions, making them essential for logos, typography, and animations. Quadratic and cubic Bezier curves are common, offering varying complexity.

Png image

PNG images, or Portable Network Graphics, are a popular file format for high-quality graphics. They support lossless compression, preserving image details without sacrificing quality. PNGs excel in transparency handling, making them ideal for logos, icons, and overlays.
